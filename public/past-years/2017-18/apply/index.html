FAQ's

What does the ECE USC do?

We put on different programs, events and workshops to help support ECE students in various ways. Some things we do include:

  • Quarterly Mentorship Program
  • Quarterly Faculty Mixer
  • Social Events (USB Decorating, DIY Ethernet Cords, etc.)
  • ECE Town Halls w/ Department Chair
  • Study Jams
  • Outreach Events

What are the qualifications to apply?

The only offical requirements we have are:

  • Will be a UCSD student during FA18, WI19, and SP19
  • Will have ~5 hours a week for Council work.
And that's it! No major, GPA, or previous work requirements needed!

Who's this opportunity good for?

Being on the Council is really great for people who want to:

  • Get involved with the ECE Department
  • Meet more ECE students in general
  • Make and see change in ECE classes or ECE student life.

What are some benefits to being on Council?

Some benefits we have seen with council members in the past are:

  • Created connections with ECE Faculty and people in the department
  • Connected with many other ECE students
  • Worked with other organizations (IEEE, HKN, etc.) in a professional setting
  • Developed leadership and organizational skills

Could I still be involved with ECE USC without being a Council member?

Yes! For the 2018-19 school year, we will have committees (Mentorship, Outreach, Project Teams, etc) that you coud join!
We also have our Mentorship Program that is offered every single quarter that you can participate in!
